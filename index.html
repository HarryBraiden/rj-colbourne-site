Our story

Born on the Shore

Gemcoast Boutique started with one simple obsession — finding swimwear as beautiful as the places we wear it.

Every piece in our collection is handpicked for quality, fit, and that effortless coastal confidence. We believe swimwear should celebrate every body — sun-kissed, salty and free. From early morning sea swims to golden-hour cocktails, we have the perfect look for every shore moment.

  • ✦Inclusive sizing across all styles
  • ♾Ethically produced with sustainable fabrics
  • 🌊Coastal-inspired curation, season after season
